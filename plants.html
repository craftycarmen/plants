Plants

Rhaphidophora Tetrasperma

Rhaphidophora tetrasperma is the most popular plant of the rhaphidophora genus. It is commonly nicknamed "mini monstera" or "monstera minima," which is why it is often mistaken for the monstera genus.

Philodendron Micans

Philodendron micans, or simply known as "micans," has a unique velvet texture. When it is sun-stressed, the dark green turns into a pink, almost red color.

Syngonium
Albo

Syngonium albos are relatively easy to care for. It is a variegated plant and its marble white color will only show if it receives enough sun. However, too much sun can cause the leaves to have too much white, which will eventually die because it lacks the chorophyll it needs to survive.

Hoya
Polyneura

Hoya polyneura received its nickname the "fishtail" hoya because of its resemblance to a fishtail. Hoyas are easy to care for—simply let the soil dry between watering and it should be golden!

Ficus
Shivereana

Ficus elastica "moonshine" shivereana is rarer in the ficus genus. The more popular ficus elastica plants are the ficus burgundy and ficus tineke. This species is known to be quite particular—once it like its spot, don't move it or else it will get moody!

Pothos
Snow Queen

Another popular plant is the pothos, such as pothos snow queen. They are very common houseplants because it is easy to care for and can grow like weeds. Other common pothos are golden pothos and marble queen pothos.
